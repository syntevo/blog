We are delighted to announce the official release of [SmartGit 26.1](https://www.smartgit.dev/).
Thank you to everyone who tested the preview and RC builds and shared feedback along the way.

SmartGit 26.1 comes with

* a broader **visual refresh** with reworked toolbar icons, improved compare colors, clearer tab and toolbar states, and a darker dark theme
* **Git 3.0 compatibility** with support for **SHA-256** repositories and **reftable** refs
* new **GitLab integration** in the **Standard window**, including merge requests, CI/CD status, direct browsing, and issue-based commit messages
* better **worktree workflows**, with clean/modified state in Branches and Graph, smoother drag and drop, and improved remove/prune handling
* **Commit Overlap** in the Graph to spot related commits, likely conflicts, and squash/reorder candidates earlier
* improved **AI workflows** with **Ask AI** custom prompts, templates, merge-commit support, and safer rewording of signed commits
* clearer **provider configuration** handling and improved **Pull vs. Pull All** behavior for multi-remote repositories
* easier discovery of the **main windows** and many additional refinements across the Standard and Log windows

[![](/assets/images/20260518-smartgit-26_1-ga-gitlab.png)](/assets/images/20260518-smartgit-26_1-ga-gitlab.png)

The GA build, **26.1.036**, adds another round of polish on top of the preview and RC cycle, including improvements for multi-remote hosting-provider setups, clearer AI-related error handling, a better fixed-width font for Japanese text, improved worktree naming, and fixes in areas such as Commit Overlap, GitLab pipeline handling, licensing, the Log, and the Windows updater.

Check out [What's New](https://www.smartgit.dev/whats-new/26_1/) for a detailed tour of the major improvements in version 26.1, or browse the [change log](https://www.smartgit.dev/changelogs/changelog-26.1.txt) for the full list of changes.

[Download SmartGit 26.1](https://www.smartgit.dev/download/)

**Note**: if you already have an existing installation of SmartGit and your license covers updates until 2026-05-18 or later, you will be upgraded automatically during the next couple of weeks.
